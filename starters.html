Starters

  • Burrata bruschetta


    Description:

    A delightful twist on the classic, our Burrata Bruschetta features

    creamy, hand-pulled burrata cheese atop a toasted, crispy baguette slice.

    Paired with vibrant heirloom tomatoes, fresh basil, and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil, this dish is finished with a sprinkle of sea salt and cracked black pepper. Rich, velvety burrata melts beautifully into the tangy tomatoes, offering a perfect balance of textures and flavors.

    Ideal as an appetizer or light starter, it's a celebration of simple, high-quality ingredients.


    Contents:

    Sugar Snap Peas, Peas, Broad Beans, Radish, Red Chilli, Mint, Parsley, Vinegar, Olive Oil, Sourdough, Burrata


  • Sweetcorn fritters


    Description:

    Golden and crispy on the outside, soft and sweet on the inside, these sweetcorn fritters are a true comfort dish. Each fritter is lightly seasoned and mixed into a fluffy batter, then fried to a perfect golden crisp. The natural sweetness of the corn shines through, complemented by hints of savory herbs and a subtle kick of spice. Served with a dollop of creamy aioli or tangy salsa, these fritters are the ideal balance of crunchy and tender. Whether as a starter, snack, or side, they’re sure to add a burst of flavor to any meal.


    Contents:

    Plain Flour, Baking Powder, Garlic powder, Milk, Eggs, Parsley, Spring Onions, Sweet Corn


  • Corn ribs


    Description:

    A delectable twist on classic corn on the cob, these corn ribs are expertly cut and grilled to perfection. Tender yet slightly crispy, each piece is bathed in a rich, smoky glaze that brings out the natural sweetness of the corn. Finished with a hint of char, the corn ribs are dusted with a blend of aromatic spices, creating a savory and slightly tangy flavor profile. Served alongside a cooling herb-infused dipping sauce, these corn ribs offer a delightful crunch with every bite, making them the perfect shareable appetizer or side dish for any meal.


    Contents:

    Corn, Sunflower or Vegetable or Rapeseed oil, Coriander, Extra-virgin Olive Oil, Lime


  • Crispy tofu


    Description:

    Our Crispy Tofu is a flavorful dish featuring tofu cubes coated in a light, crispy batter and fried to golden perfection. The exterior offers a satisfying crunch, while the interior remains soft and tender. Served with a tangy dipping sauce, each bite delivers a perfect balance of textures and flavors. It's an ideal choice for those seeking a plant-based option full of savory goodness. Enjoy this crispy delight as an appetizer or a main course that will leave you craving more.


    Contents:

    Block Firm Tofu, Cornflour, Garlic, Smoked Paprika, Sea Salt, Black Pepper, Vegtable Oil
